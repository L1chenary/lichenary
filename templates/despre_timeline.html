Project Timeline & Events

July 1, 2025

Official launch of the Lichenary project

The website went live, opening the door to the lichen enthusiast community.

July 30, 2025

First meeting with volunteers

An introductory session was held to guide new volunteers on how to photograph lichens and upload their observations.

August 1, 2025

First field trip – Buzău

Participants explored Crâng Park, practicing lichen identification and contributing to the project’s first local observations.

August 13, 2025

First field trip – Cluj

A visit to Central Park provided opportunities to observe lichens and assess air quality using visual indicators.

August 18, 2025

First field trip – Constanța

Urban observations were carried out, with a focus on species diversity and local environmental conditions.

August 21, 2025

First field trip – Bucharest

Explorers visited Herăstrău Park to collect data and test live uploads directly from the field.
