Gheorghe Rareș-Răzvan

Founder

Hey! I'm Rareș, also a sophomore in Buzău, Romania. I'm passionate about sciences – be it earth science, chemistry, or biology, I always try to stay curious and find out more.

I started this project alongside my twin sister, Carla, as a way to promote environmental sciences in my community and to raise awareness on the impacts of climate change.

Lichens are (in a way) at the limit between plants and fungi, and their fragility makes for a perfect environmental project and also for interesting metaphors.

That being said, I wish we, through this project, will be able to make a difference and have a lasting impact on the planet.
